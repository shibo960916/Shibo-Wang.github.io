About Me

Hi, I'm Gongye Liu, an incoming Ph.D. student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Prof. Wenhan Luo. My current research focus on GenAI. I'm particularly interested in diffusion/flow-matching generative models.

Prior to that, I obtained my M.Sc degree(2022-2025) in Artificial Intelligence from Tsinghua University, supervised by Professor Yujiu Yang. I received my B.Eng degree(2018-2022) in Beihang University.
